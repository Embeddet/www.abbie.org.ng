4. Advanced Professional Chef Diploma (Cuisine, Baking, Pattiserie and Restaurant Management)

The Advanced Professional Chef Diploma program (Cuisine, Baking, Pattiserie and Restaurant Management) is a well-balanced course designed to equip you with fundamental and advance skills that will make you a chef, baker/pastry chef, hospitality manager and restaurateur.

We pride ourselves on our ability to provide personal instruction and hands-on training from our seasoned chef instructors in a comfortable, well-equipped atmosphere, and giving individual attention to each student’s individual career and personal goals.

The program is divided into five (5) phases,

Phase one (1) - Culinary Fundamentals and Food Production

Phase two (2) - Baking and Pastry Arts

Phase three (3) - Restaurant and Hospitality Service Training

Phase four (4) - Professionalism, Life skill and Entrepreneurial Training

Phase five (5) - Internship

Each of these phases helps you to develop skills in food preparation, production, presentation, service, baking and pastry arts, restaurant and hospitality management and entrepreneurship.

Duration: 9 months
Internship: 5 Weeks (compulsory)
