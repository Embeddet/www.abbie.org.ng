OUR SERVICES

RESTAURANT: HONEY PLUM

Honey Plum restaurant is the private dining restaurant set up to hone the skills of the students of Abbie Culinary Arts Academy & Hospitality Academy and
to expose them to real life restaurant operation while preparing them for the food service industry.
The dining style is à la carte menu, using the best of local fresh ingredients.
The restaurant is opened Mondays – Saturdays 2pm - 7pm.
