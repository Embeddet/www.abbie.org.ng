About Abbie Culinary Arts Academy & Hospitality

Abbie Culinary Arts Academy & Hospitality is the most prestigious culinary school in Nigeria, committed to providing the highest standard of culinary education that beats global standard.
Our mission is to nurture and equip our students with exceptional culinary skills for a successful career in the global Food Industry.

Who We Are

Established in 2014, Abbie Culinary Arts Academy & Hospitality is one of the top centres in Nigeria offering international standard, yet affordable culinary education. We have produced food leaders through our well-balanced curriculum embedded with academic, social, entrepreneurial and professional skills.
